    Frequently Asked Question

    Are you Doctors?

    No. We are not Doctors, we are Pharmacists and strongly recommend consulting a Medical Doctor regarding any health concern. We strictly provide pharmaceutical services.

    Can I call instead of text chat?

    Yes. We have provisions for audio and video chat with our Pharmacists. However, while the 30-minutes text chat is free, audio and video consultation is a paid service.

    Do you deliver prescription drugs?

    Yes, we do. However, we need to see a prescription obtained from a Doctor before we can deliver prescription drugs.

    How authentic are the medications...?

    We are dedicated to delivering only quality and authentic medications. To achieve this, we have partnered with only pharmacies that are fully licensed and regulated by the Pharmacists’ Council of Nigeria. (PCN), as well as agencies regulating products.

    When can I place orders?

    Orders can be placed at any time of the day using any of our platforms.

    Can I order for a family member?

    Yes. However, indicate who the drug is for as this information is important for your database and can help us provide professional recommendations.

    Is it safe to order the Drug online...?

    We are confident to say definitely Yes. We are dedicated to delivering only quality and authentic medications, which is why we have workforce of qualified and fully licensed Pharmacists who work for E-Medix Store and also, we partner with fully licensed pharmacy stores to ensure you get the best value for your money.

    Can I cancel/edit my order?

    Yes, you can cancel or edit your order. However, once an order has been paid for, it will be locked and you will be UNABLE to edit or cancel your order.

    What areas do you serve?

    We currently deliver within Abuja, however, we have special provisions for delivery outside Abuja while we are working to expand our service area.

    What happens if I miss a dose?

    Take the medicine as soon as you can, but skip the missed dose if it is almost time for your next dose. Do not take two doses at one time.

    What to avoid while taking Drugs.

    Follow your doctor's instructions about any restrictions on food, beverages, or activity.

    What if i am on ongoing drugs?

    Tell your doctor about all your other medicines you are taking
